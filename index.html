Quality legal counsel and cost control are key variables in sustaining your company's outstanding track record.
Intellectual Property - High Tech Law

  • Intellectual property law intertwines contract, licensing, ownership, privacy, infringement, tax, restrictions on trade regulations, and other issues.

  • High technology law is much more than a collection of legal disciplines, it is understanding the client and the client's business.

  • An effective legal adviser is a proactive business liaison bringing a complimentary perspective to the planning stage, strategy sessions and negotiation table.  A lawyer who does not understand and follow the industry cannot be an effective adviser.
Emerging Corporations
  • Emerging high tech corporations, hoping to avoid the fixed costs associated with hiring a full time attorney, often retain a law firm to provide a full range of legal services.  

  • To reduce legal expenses, management often assumes responsibilities for many legal issues associated with doing business.  This is a time consuming method.  One for which some members of management may not be specifically trained, and one which may lead to serious oversights.

  • Consequently, law firms are called upon in certain instances after problems arise.  Despite the corporation's best efforts at monitoring outside counsels' billing, corporations often discover that this method entails significant yearly legal expenses.
"Legal Maintenance Organization"
WW@Law can best be described as a "Legal Maintenance Organization."  Much like an HMO which seeks to care for immediate health needs and prevent future illness, a Legal Maintenance Organization reduces legal and business expenses by addressing and minimizing legal concerns.

We combine the flexibility of project base or per hour billing with the advantages of a full time attorney.
